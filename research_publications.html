Research / Publications (Past & Present)

The SiX Research Group is devoted to advancing communication and accessibility for individuals with Locked-in Syndrome (LIS). The team consists of researchers and graduate students who explore the intersection of design, technology, and healthcare to develop innovative solutions that enhance the quality of life for individuals with LIS. Below are highlights of completed and ongoing research projects, showcasing the team's contributions to the field.
